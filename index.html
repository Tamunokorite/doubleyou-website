Get to Know Us

DoubleYou is not just a company; it's a revolution in educational gaming.

Founded in 2019 at Covenant University by Israel Yabkwa, DoubleYou started with a simple yet powerful idea: to make learning fun, engaging, and rewarding.

From humble beginnings, organizing short, fun tech boot camps for early graduates, DoubleYou has evolved into a beacon of innovation in the realm of educational gaming.

Our mission is to make learning fun, engaging, and rewarding, while also fostering a culture of lifelong learning.

What we do

DoubleYou pioneers the fusion of education and gaming, creating innovative platforms that captivate and inspire learners of all ages.

Our flagship activities include:

  • Edu-gaming Quizzes

    We develop engaging quizzes across various subjects and reward participants with cash prizes, making learning both challenging and rewarding.

  • Web and Mobile Apps:

    Stay tuned as we prepare to launch our groundbreaking web and mobile apps, redefining learning with user-centric design. Doubleyou apps will be your ultimate destination for knowledge and fun, anytime, anywhere.

  • Battle of Brains
